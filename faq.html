Frequently Asked Questions

Your common questions, answered.

AuraSync X is built on modern web technologies and works best on the latest versions of desktop browsers like Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, and Microsoft Edge. While it may function on other browsers, we recommend these for the best performance, especially for the 3D visualizer.

Yes, AuraSync X is completely free to use. It is a passion project, and the goal is to provide a high-quality tool to the community. If you enjoy the app, please consider a small donation to help cover server and development costs.

The 3D Music Visualizer primarily supports .MP3 files for local uploads. The MP4 Video Player supports .MP4 files. Both applications also support direct streaming from URLs, provided they link to a compatible audio or video stream.

Your privacy is our top priority. AuraSync X is a fully client-side application. This means no files or personal data are ever uploaded to our servers. All processing happens locally in your browser. For more details, please read our full Privacy Policy.

The best way to report issues or make suggestions is through our GitHub Issues page. This allows us to track feedback publicly and transparently. You can also reach out via the methods on our Contact page.
