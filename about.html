About AuraSync X

The story, the tech, and the future of your favorite media hub.

Our Story

AuraSync X began as a passion project by Stephen Alfred, a developer based in Nova Scotia, Canada. Inspired by the classic visualizers of the early 2000s and a love for modern web technology, the goal was to create a beautiful, private, and powerful media player that runs entirely in the browser. What started as a simple particle visualizer grew into a comprehensive suite, all while holding true to the core principle: your data is your own.

Technology Stack

This project stands on the shoulders of giants. It's built with a focus on modern, open-source technologies that allow for a fast and responsive user experience.

Project Roadmap

The journey is far from over. Here are some of the features and improvements planned for the future:

  • More advanced visualization options and greater customization.
  • Support for additional audio and video formats.
  • Improved playlist import/export functionality.
  • Light theme for enhanced accessibility.
  • Community-sourced radio station lists.
